Overview

Purpose

This site will prove to drive traffic to the eSports orginisation that a few friends and I have started. This will serve as a website that can coherently adn professionally get across who we are, what we do, and why we do it.

Audience

Our audience consists of 3 parts. Firstly a place for fans to find information on us. Secondly a place for players looking for an org for their team to find a potential home. Fianally a place to direct potential sponsors to or allow them to discover us.

About Us

What is Helios?
We’re a grassroots org for players and content creators with a staff passionate about esports. Officially established in 2023, with 15 years combined experience in industry, we hope to provide a nurturing environment for players and creators to grow and find success in esports and beyond.
Looking to call Helios Esports home?
Helios is looking for players in Halo Infinite, Apex Legends, and Rocket League! Not a professional gamer? We have other positions open such as team manager, community member, and content creator! We have a team on hand to help you grow your following and outreach.
